A redesign of the classic club master frame, the Navigator is a frame that pushes the boundary of traditional silhouette through form and proportions. It deviates from tradition with square shape lenses and additions of thicker acetate and metal detailing. The floating wire core details and the metal and acetate beveling creates a true elevation to a timeless classic.
