The Titan is a bold, rectangular frame crafted from premium 8mm thick beveled acetate, featuring a prominent bevel along the top-front for an unmistakable look. The signature enamel-filled, 3D "O" logo adorns the temples, while the engraved lines on the temple tips provide a secure and comfortable fit.
